Warung Jakarta Colour Palette
Core colours

1. Warung Jakarta Red — Primary
#E24A34

Use for:

Main buttons and CTAs
Headings and highlights
Active navigation
Icons and borders
Promotional elements

2. Pure White — Secondary
#FFFFFF

Use for:

Main page background
Cards and menu boxes
Button text on red backgrounds
Clean spacing and contrast

3. Dark Coffee Brown — Text Neutral
#3B241B

Use for:

Main body text
Menu prices
Footer text
Dark outlines and subtle brand details

White: 70%
Warung Jakarta Red: 20%
Dark Coffee Brown: 10%

Font System
1. Main Heading Font
Bricolage Grotesque

Recommended weights:

Bold 700: Hero headings
SemiBold 600: Section headings
Medium 500: Small card headings

Examples:

font-family: "Bricolage Grotesque", sans-serif;
font-weight: 700;

Use for:

“Plan Your Meal”
Menu category titles
Promotional headings
Important section titles
2. Body and Menu Font
DM Sans

Recommended weights:

Regular 400: Body text and descriptions
Medium 500: Navigation and labels
SemiBold 600: Buttons and menu prices
Bold 700: Important totals and CTAs
font-family: "DM Sans", sans-serif;
font-weight: 400;

Use for:

Menu descriptions
Navigation
Buttons
Prices
Forms
Meal planner information
3. Accent Font Style
DM Sans Medium Italic
font-family: "DM Sans", sans-serif;
font-weight: 500;
font-style: italic;

Use sparingly for:

“Fresh. Authentic. Welcoming.”
Short taglines
Small promotional messages
Heritage or emotional statements
Recommended Typography Hierarchy
Element	Font	Weight
Hero heading	Bricolage Grotesque	700
Section heading	Bricolage Grotesque	600
Card title	Bricolage Grotesque	500–600
Body text	DM Sans	400
Navigation	DM Sans	500
Menu price	DM Sans	600–700
Button text	DM Sans	600
Accent tagline	DM Sans Italic	500
Final brand combination

Bricolage Grotesque + DM Sans

It gives Warung Jakarta a modern, friendly and approachable F&B identity while keeping the menu easy to read.
